Privacy Policy 
    Personal identification information

    We collect personal identification information from users, when users log in to the wallet app or fill contact form. When you log in to the wallet app with your Google Account, your email address will be saved to Firebase Authentication. Users may or may not provide an email address, when contacting us via the contact form.
          
    Users may, however, visit our website or wallet app anonymously.
          
    We will collect personal identification information from Users only if they voluntarily submit such information to us. Users can always refuse to supply personally identification information, except that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website or wallet app related activities.
    
    Web browser cookies
    
    Our website uses cookies to retain website state, no personal data is saved. The wallet app, may retain your account related data until you log out.
    
    Non-personal identification information
    
    No data about the user activities will be stored, when interacting with the website (excluding the contact form).
    
    When interaction with the wallet, non-personal information about the users is stored to Firebase Realtime Database depending on user activities. You may, check the exact types of data stored in Firebase Realtime Database from the source code of Dirsh available on Github (https://github.com/JarmoKukkola/DirshPlatform).
    
    How we use collected information
    
    Dirsh collects and uses Users personal information for the following purposes:
          
    To personalize user experience
    
    Google log in used to identify your personal Dirsh account.
    
    To send email correspondes
          
    The email address Users provide for feedback processing, will only be used to send them information and updates pertaining to their request. It may also be used to respond to their inquiries, and/or other requests or questions. If at any time the User would like to unsubscribe from receiving future emails, User may contact us via our contact form.  Email addresses collected from wallet app are used only for identifying your account and sending security related warnings. Email addresses collected from contact form are only used for correspondence and are subsequently deleted.
    
    How your personal information is stored
    
    Your personal information is stored on the Firebase Authentication and Firebase Realtime Database systems. We take serious effort keep your information safe. We protect the data with passwords and two factor authentication. We do not take any backups of your personal identification information.
    
    Messages and contact information send via our contact form are deleted from our database within 6 weeks.
    
    Your Google log in related data will remain in Firebase Authentication until you delete it yourself from the wallet app via "Settings-Delete Account".
    
    How we protect your information
    
    We adopt appropriate data collection, storage and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure or destruction of your personal email, and data stored on our data storages.
          
    Sensitive and private data exchange between the our servers and its Users happens over a SSL secured communication channel and is encrypted and protected with digital signatures.

    Firebase Privacy Policy
    
    Dirsh uses Firebase Realtime Database and Firebase Authentication to provide our services. If you decide to use our services, you have to also consent to Firebase Privacy Policy (https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy/ https://firebase.google.com/terms/analytics) and Terms of Service (https://firebase.google.com/terms/).
          
    Compliance with children's online privacy protection act
    
    Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. For that reason, we never collect or maintain information at our website or wallet app from those we actually know are under 18, and no part of our website or wallet app are structured to attract anyone under 18.
    
    Right to Be Forgotten
    
    If you have contacted us via the contact form, you may request removal of your messages via the contact form. Please, state your email, so we can identify, which messages are yours. If you have used the wallet app to log in, basic information about your Google account will be registered to Firebase Authentication. You are able to delete your login data with "Delete Account" option in the wallet. The transaction history and account balances will not be deleted from our servers. If you choose to delete your account, you are not anymore able to access your tokens or transaction history of your account. In addition, we are not able to identify, which account is related to your social media account. However, sending tokens via REST API remains active, if you created API keys prior to deletion of your account. If you choose to log in to the wallet after deletion of the account, a new account will created with a new account number.

    Right to Be Informed

    You may request a summary of collected personal data via the contact form at https://dirsh.com/contact. Please, state your email address, because otherwise we are not able to identify you. We will send you an email to confirm that you are in control of the email address.
